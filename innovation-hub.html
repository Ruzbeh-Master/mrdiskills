ISAC.SI — The Capability Intelligence Platform

ISAC (Integrated System Assistance Core) is a proprietary AI-powered capability intelligence platform developed by MRDI to measure, analyze, and enhance real-world skill execution.

At its core, ISAC leverages the Learning Capability Index (LCI) — evaluating learning velocity, practical application, adaptability, and execution consistency. Currently validated under SSIP 2.0.

  • Evaluates learning velocity and practical application
  • Personalized module assignments and capability tracking
  • Validated under SSIP 2.0
  • Scalable across education and workforce ecosystems
