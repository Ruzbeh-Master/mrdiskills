3 Tier Skill Structure

Engineering Is More Than Degrees

Having a degree may get you into interviews, but skills are what land you the job. MRDI presents the 3-Tier Skill Structure for Engineers — designed to connect your academics, practical learning, and career growth.

01

Tier 1 — Soft Skills

Communication, teamwork, problem-solving — the foundation every engineer needs to succeed in any workplace environment.

02

Tier 2 — Core Skills

Using tools, reading manuals, workshop operations — the practical skills that make you immediately useful in any automotive workshop.

03

Tier 3 — Advanced Skills

Kaizen, Lean, Kanban, AI, and prototyping — the cutting-edge skills that set you apart and make you a future-ready engineer.
