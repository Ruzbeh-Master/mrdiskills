Introducing Learning Capability Index 1:09

The Learning Capability Index maps how skill behaves.

ISAC.XP looks at capability as a pattern, not a single test score. The profile is designed around practical signals such as reasoning, pace, consistency, recall, and confidence so a learner can understand where they are strong and where focused practice will help.

LogicHow clearly the learner reasons through a problem and chooses the next step.
VelocityHow quickly the learner can move from understanding to useful action.
StabilityHow well the learner performs when the task becomes uncertain or pressured.
ConsistencyHow reliably the learner repeats good process across different attempts.
RecallHow available previous learning becomes when a real task demands it.
ConfidenceHow accurately the learner can express, defend, and apply what they know.
