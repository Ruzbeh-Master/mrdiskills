Our Foundation Story

From Passion to Profession

Not everyone gets a clear path to success. I was once a school dropout, struggling to keep up with academics. But I found my way — not through textbooks alone, but by getting my hands dirty, repairing motorcycles, and eventually building them.

That journey led to something bigger — Master Research & Development Institute (MRDI). MRDI is built for students like YOU — students who want to learn by doing, who want real skills, not just certificates.

We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and hands-on expertise, preparing you for real-world careers in the automotive and EV industries.

  • 6-month Skill Development Program in Automotive & EV Technology
  • Hands-on training in workshops, working on real vehicles
  • Industry-ready experience to help you land jobs in top automotive firms

Engineering Achievement

All Wheel Drive Motorcycle Build | Maruti 800 Engine Transformation

What happens when you put a car engine in a motorcycle and drive both wheels? We took on the ultimate challenge!

Transforming a Maruti 800 engine into an all-wheel-drive motorcycle — from concept to reality, this build pushed the limits of engineering, design, and innovation.

Watch how our students tackled frame design, transmission tweaks, and power distribution to bring this beast to life. At MRDI, we don't just teach — we build, innovate, and create.
